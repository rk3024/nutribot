What is Nutribot?

Nutribot is your intelligent WhatsApp companion that helps you make informed decisions about packaged foods. Using advanced AI technology powered by ChatGPT, Nutribot analyzes food packaging images to identify potentially harmful ingredients.

Simply snap a picture of any food package's ingredient list and send it to Nutribot on WhatsApp. Within seconds, you'll receive a detailed analysis highlighting any concerning ingredients and their potential health impacts.
